Watch Me Grow

The Watch Me Grow (WMG) web app is a tool designed for you as a parent to track how well your child is developing. The WMG app uses two questionnaires: Q-CHAT is a quick referral guide for parents to indicate any concerns about their child’s language and social skills. PEDS asks questions about your child’s overall development. Parents are encouraged to use the WMG app at key stages in your child’s development at 6, 12, 18, 24, 36, and 48 months of age. If any concerns are flagged on either of these screening tools it is important to make an appointment to see your GP or another health professional.

  • Q-CHAT (Quantitative Checklist for Autism in Toddlers) is validated for use from 18 to 30 months
  • Parents can complete PEDS (Parents’ Evaluation of Developmental Status) from the age of 6 months which is used internationally and across Australia (e.g. NSW Personal Health Record).
